How to use

Set your times: Enter the number of seconds for each hang, rest period, and how many sets you want to complete.

Start training: Press START to begin. The timer counts down your hang, then automatically switches to rest, repeating for the number of sets you set.

Sounds: A high beep signals the start of a hang; a lower tone signals rest. Disable sounds with the checkbox above if training in a quiet environment.

Pause & reset: PAUSE freezes the timer mid-session. RESET returns to the beginning without changing your settings.

Settings are saved automatically between visits, so you can close and reopen the app without losing your configuration.
